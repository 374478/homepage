About

Hi, I'm Ffin. I'm twenty, and I'm a software engineer with experience in Python, Perl, HTMl/CSS, and Arduinos. One of my main personal projects was to create accurate simulations of roller coaster control systems using Arduino, and for the Arduinos to communicate with the NoLimits 2 telemetry.

Some of my personal hobbies include DJing, 3D printing, going on world-class roller coasters, and perfumery.
